Challenge: Colorful creature

The coolest creature : Deers

Deers are the coolest creatures because they are calm and friendly,
Fawns can stand and walk just 20 minutes after being born.
Deers can run as fast as 30-40 miles per hour and male deers grow antlers every year and shed them.
In Nara-Deer-Park-Japan, you can feed and pet deers aswell as meet other animals!
